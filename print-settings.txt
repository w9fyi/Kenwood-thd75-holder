Recommended slicer settings

Material: PLA
Layer height: 0.20 mm
Perimeters/walls: 3
Top/bottom layers: 5
Infill: 20% gyroid or grid
Supports: Off
Brim: Off unless your printer has bed-adhesion issues
Print orientation: Flat on the bottom face

Optional:
- Add thin stick-on rubber pads under the four corners.
- Add adhesive felt strips to the interior rails if you want a softer fit.
